Health Data Research UK (HDR UK) IS UK's national institute for health data science,
with a vision that every health interaction and research endeavour will be enhanced by access to large scale data and advanced analytics.
Their gateway streamlines the end-to-end user journey to search, discover and request access to the wealth of health and associated Datasets.
