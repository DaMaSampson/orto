An open dataset of <span class="font-bold">retinal parafoveal optical coherence tomography angiography</span> (OCTA) 3Ã—3-mm images (RTVue-XR Avanti, Optovue) from 11 participants (both eyes) not presenting ocular disease, age 44-59) with associated ground truth manual segmentations. The dataset includes maximum-intensity projection images. For each OCTA image, five sub-images were extracted and binarised. The OCTA dataset is available <a href="https://doi.org/10.7488/ds/2729">here</a>.
