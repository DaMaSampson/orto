Prof. Andrew Lam graduated from the then Hong Kong Polytechnic (HKP) with a Professional Diploma in Optometry.
After working in a private optometric practice for two years, he returned to HKP working as a Research Assistant and later obtained his Master and PhD degrees from the Department of Optometry, University of Bradford, in the UK.
He is now the Programme Leader of the Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Optometry at the School of Optometry, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.
He was a recipient of the Department's Teaching Excellence Award in 2002 and 2008.
He received the Garland W. Clay Award from the American Academy of Optometry in 2016.
He is a Fellow of the American Academy of Optometry.
