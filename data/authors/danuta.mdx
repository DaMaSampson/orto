Danuta (Danka) Sampson is an internationally trained translational researcher jointly appointed between the Lions Eye Institute and the University of Western Australia. She designs and constructs non-invasive optics-based imaging instruments and develops standardised workflows for ophthalmic image and data analysis. Her long-term career goal is to equip clinicians, biologists and scientists with standardised hardware and software tools to study retinal neuro-vascular units in health and disease, prevent and monitor retinal photoreceptors and vascular diseases, and make a real difference to patients.
